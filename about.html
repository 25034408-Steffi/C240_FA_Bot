Our Mission

In an age of information overload, NewsLens Analyser helps you analyze and summarize news articles so you can stay informed without spending hours reading.

Built by a team of RP students being graded and AI subscription payers, NewsLens Analyser combines information processing with intuitive design to deliver effective news analysis.
